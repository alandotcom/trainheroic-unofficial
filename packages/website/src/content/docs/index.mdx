## What you can ask

Coaches query rosters, workouts, teams, analytics, and messages; athletes look
up history, PRs, working maxes, and today's program. Claude confirms before it
publishes or sends anything.

## Export your workout history

Want your data as a spreadsheet? [Download your full history](export/) as CSV,
JSON, or text. You sign in right in your browser, and your login never leaves
it.
